Our Services

Comprehensive furniture solutions for homes and businesses

Custom Furniture Design

We work with you to design and create furniture that perfectly fits your space, style, and needs. From initial sketches to final product, we ensure every detail matches your vision.

Furniture Manufacturing

Using quality Chapa Mbao wood and other premium materials, our skilled craftsmen build durable, beautiful furniture that will last for generations.

Furniture Restoration

Bring new life to your cherished furniture pieces. Our restoration services can repair damage, replace parts, and refinish surfaces to restore their original beauty.

Delivery & Installation

We handle the delivery and professional installation of your furniture, ensuring it's properly set up in your home or office.

Our Process

  1. Consultation: We discuss your needs, preferences, and budget.
  2. Design: We create designs based on your requirements.
  3. Approval: You review and approve the design.
  4. Crafting: Our artisans build your furniture.
  5. Delivery: We deliver and install the finished product.
