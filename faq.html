Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our furniture and services

General Questions

What types of wood do you use?

We primarily use Chapa Mbao wood, known for its durability and beautiful grain patterns. We also work with other quality hardwoods like mahogany, oak, and teak based on customer preferences and project requirements.

How long does it take to make custom furniture?

The timeframe depends on the complexity of the design and our current workload. Simple pieces may take 2-3 weeks, while more complex custom designs can take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide an estimated timeline during our consultation.

Do you offer delivery and installation?

Yes, we offer delivery and professional installation services for all our furniture. Delivery charges depend on your location. We ensure your furniture is properly assembled and positioned in your space.

Pricing & Payment

How is pricing determined for custom furniture?

Pricing is based on several factors including: the type and amount of materials used, complexity of the design, size of the piece, and any special finishes or details requested. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and mobile payments. For larger orders, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion before delivery.

Do you offer financing or payment plans?

For substantial orders, we can arrange payment plans. This is discussed on a case-by-case basis during the consultation phase.

Care & Maintenance

How do I care for my Chapa Mbao furniture?

We recommend:

  • Dust regularly with a soft, dry cloth
  • Avoid placing in direct sunlight or near heat sources
  • Use coasters under drinks and hot items
  • Clean spills immediately with a damp cloth
  • Use furniture polish specifically for wood every few months
We provide specific care instructions with each piece.

Do you offer warranty on your furniture?

Yes, we offer a 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects. This covers issues related to craftsmanship and materials. Normal wear and tear or damage from improper care is not covered.

What if I need repairs after purchase?

We offer repair services for our furniture. Contact us with details of the issue, and we'll provide an assessment and quote for the repair work needed.

Still have questions?

If you didn't find the answer you were looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us directly. Our team is happy to help with any additional questions you may have.
